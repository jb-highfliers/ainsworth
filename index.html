About Ainsworth English Wines

In 2018, after one of the hottest summers on record, brothers Ben and Jeremy Brown realised that the time had come. English wines - once a thing of mere mythology - were here to stay. And so they decided to celebrate them accordingly, setting out on a mission to introduce them to the nation. And so Ainsworth English Wines was born...

Our Mission Statement

  1. To celebrate the best of English wines
  2. To go above-and-beyond with our recommendations: we are sommeliers, not just salespeople
  3. To make our system as easy-to-use as possible
